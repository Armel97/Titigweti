Events and competitions

In addition to weekly club meetings, Titigweti English Club organizes yearly events and competitions to bring members together and create a friendly environment where beauty and knowledge are celebrated. Read more about any of the events on their respective pages, and contact us if you require more information.

Lol Night

Lol Night is an event organized by Titigweti English Club. It is an opportunity for students of the other entities to join with members of the club to entertain and learn through through various performances offered for the occasion.
